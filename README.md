# Verdant Hour: Cozy Gardening Simulator

Welcome to **Verdant Hour**, a relaxing gardening simulator where each in-game hour brings new weather, growth patterns, and tiny ecosystem surprises.

Build your dream garden, crossbreed whimsical plants, and keep your little biome healthy through changing conditions.

## Features

- Real-time-style hourly growth cycles
- Dynamic weather shifts (sun, mist, drizzle, storm)
- Soil moisture and nutrient balancing
- Seed rarities and hybrid plant discovery
- Garden journal with seasonal milestones
- Chill progression focused on planning and care

## Gameplay Loop

1. Prepare beds and choose seeds.
2. Water and tune soil conditions.
3. Track hourly growth events.
4. Harvest, replant, and unlock new species.
5. Expand your greenhouse and decorative spaces.

## Design Goals

- Calm, low-pressure gardening sessions
- Meaningful but simple resource management
- Emergent stories from plant and weather interactions

## Roadmap

- Companion pollinators
- Greenhouse climate automation
- Seasonal community events
- Photo mode and garden sharing
